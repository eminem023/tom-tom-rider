How to Update TomTom Rider Maps

The TomTom Rider GPS is designed for motorcyclists who need reliable and scenic navigation. To get the most out of your device, it's important to keep your maps updated with the latest roads, routes, and safety information.

Benefits of Updating Your TomTom Rider

  • Improved route accuracy and turn-by-turn directions
  • Updated points of interest for bikers (gas, rest stops, etc.)
  • Optimized scenic and hilly route algorithms
  • Safety alerts and updated speed zone information

Steps to Update TomTom Rider via Wi-Fi

  • Power on your TomTom Rider device
  • Connect it to a stable Wi-Fi network
  • Tap Settings â†’ Updates & New Items
  • Follow on-screen prompts to download available updates
  • Stay connected until the process completes

Steps to Update via MyDrive Connect (USB)

  • Install MyDrive Connect on your Windows or Mac computer
  • Connect the Rider GPS using the original USB cable
  • Sign in and wait for the device to be recognized
  • Select updates and click Install
  • Do not disconnect your device during the update

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure device has sufficient storage space
  • Use a strong and uninterrupted internet connection
  • Restart the device if updates fail or freeze
  • Update the MyDrive Connect software if outdated

Conclusion

Keeping your TomTom Rider GPS updated ensures a safe, enjoyable, and efficient riding experience. Whether you're exploring scenic backroads or commuting daily, updated maps help you avoid delays and stay on course.
